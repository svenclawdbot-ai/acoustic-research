## 🔌 What You'll Build

**Breadboard prototype:**
- ESP32-S3 drives AD9833 DDS → sine wave to soil
- 4-electrode Wenner array (M6 rods, 50mm spacing)
- OPA1641 TIA converts soil current to voltage
- ESP32-S3 ADC reads voltage → impedance → moisture %
- DS18B20 reads soil temperature
- TP4056 + 18650 makes it portable

**First test:** `cal-dry` in dry compost → `cal-wet` in moist compost → live moisture readings.

---

## 📝 Notes

- **Pinecil power:** Needs 65W USB-C PD or 12–24V DC. Your laptop charger probably won't cut it. Use a 65W+ phone charger or old laptop brick.
- **18650 safety:** Never short the terminals. The protected cell prevents over-discharge, but still treat it with respect.
- **M6 rod:** Buy 1m length, cut four 100mm pieces with junior hacksaw. Deburr with sandpaper.
- **Flux for stainless:** Standard rosin won't wet stainless well. Use RMA flux paste (Amtech NC-559) or the flux pen on the BOM. Or skip soldering and use jubilee clips.

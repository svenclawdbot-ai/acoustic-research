# References — Sparse Viscoelastic Elastography Research

## Foundational Papers (Pre-2000)

1. **Ophir J, Céspedes I, Ponnekanti H, Yazdi Y, Li X.** (1991)
   - *Elastography: A quantitative method for imaging the elasticity of biological tissues.*
   - Ultrasonic Imaging, 13(2):111-134.
   - DOI: 10.1177/016173469101300201
   - **Significance:** THE foundational paper establishing elastography concept

2. **Céspedes I, Ophir J, Ponnekanti H, Maklad N.** (1992)
   - *Elastography: elasticity imaging using ultrasound with application to muscle and breast in vivo.*
   - Ultrasonic Imaging, 14(2):113-134.

3. **Yamakoshi Y, Sato J, Sato T.** (1990)
   - *Ultrasonic imaging of internal vibration of soft tissue under forced vibration.*
   - IEEE Transactions on Ultrasonics, Ferroelectrics, and Frequency Control, 37(2):45-53.

4. **Sarvazyan AP, Rudenko OV, Swanson SD, Fowlkes JB, Emelianov SY.** (1998)
   - *Shear wave elasticity imaging: a new ultrasonic technology of medical diagnostics.*
   - Ultrasound in Medicine & Biology, 24(9):1419-1435.
   - **Significance:** Established shear wave propagation approach

5. **Parker KJ, Lerner RM.** (1992)
   - *Sonoelasticity of organs: Shear waves ring a bell.*
   - Journal of Ultrasound in Medicine, 11(8):387-392.

     - Passband: 0-10MHz (ripple <0.5dB)
     - Stopband: >62.5MHz (Nyquist/2, attenuation >60dB)

2. **Bandpass Filter for Echo Extraction**

   - Plot magnitude response and group delay

3. **Matched Filter for Pulse Compression**

   - Design a matched filter to maximise SNR for reflected echoes
   - Impulse response = time-reversed, conjugated transmitted pulse
   - Compute output SNR improvement vs unfiltered reception
   - Show how matched filter compresses pulse in time

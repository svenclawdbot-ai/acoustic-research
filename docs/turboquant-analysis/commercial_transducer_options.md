## SPECIFICATIONS TO CONFIRM WHEN ORDERING

Regardless of supplier, specify:

| Parameter | Your Target | Tolerance |
|-----------|-------------|-----------|
| Centre frequency | 3.5 MHz | ±5% |
| Aperture diameter | 19 mm | ±0.5 mm |
| Focal depth | 40 mm | ±2 mm |
| -6 dB bandwidth | >80% | — |
| Pulse duration | <1 μs | — |
| Connector | BNC or SMA | — |
| Housing | Metal | — |

**Ask for:**
- Impedance vs frequency plot
- Beam profile at focal plane
- Pulse-echo waveform
- Calibration certificate (if available)

### Today's Challenge

**Problem:** Real ultrasound arrays have limited receiver counts (8-16 elements) and fixed geometry. How does aperture limitation affect parameter identifiability?

**Tasks:**

2. **Bayesian Identifiability Study** (90 min)

   - Compute posterior curvature (Fisher information proxy) for each parameter
   - Quantify: how does element count affect G₀ vs G∞ uncertainty?

3. **Optimal Design Insight** (30 min)
   - Based on results, derive a rule-of-thumb for minimum elements needed
   - Consider: clinical requirement is ±10% accuracy on G₀
   - Deliverable: 1-paragraph design guideline with caveats

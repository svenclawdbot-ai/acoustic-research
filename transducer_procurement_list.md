## QUALITY CONTROL CHECKPOINTS

### After Each Phase:

**Phase 1 (Tools):**
- [ ] Vacuum chamber holds -0.08 MPa for 10 minutes
- [ ] Scale reads accurately (test with known weight)
- [ ] PZT crystals arrived undamaged

**Phase 2 (Build):**
- [ ] Backing: No visible voids under microscope
- [ ] Matching layer: 0.11 mm ± 0.02 mm thickness
- [ ] Housing: Lens centered, no light leaks

**Phase 3 (Test):**
- [ ] Impedance: 20–30 Ω @ 3.5 MHz
- [ ] Pulse-echo: Visible echo from flat reflector
- [ ] Bandwidth: >100% (-6 dB)

## RISK ASSESSMENT

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| **PZT delivery delay** | Medium | High | Order 2–3 suppliers simultaneously |
| **Vacuum chamber insufficient** | Low | High | Buy 2-gallon minimum, test immediately |
| **Epoxy voids** | Medium | Medium | Degas longer (5+ minutes), thin pours |
| **Matching layer wrong thickness** | Medium | Medium | Caliper check wet, scrape and redo if needed |
| **First build fails** | High | Low | Expected — budget for 3 PZT discs |
| **Scope/generator inadequate** | Low | Medium | USB options work; upgrade later |

;; FLYBACK HV SUPPLY
;; =================
;;
;;         +12V RAIL
;;            |
;;       +----v-------------------------+
;;       |                             |
;;       |    UC3843 Pinout:           |
;;       |    1: COMP (feedback)        |
;;       |    2: VFB (voltage feedback) ←──[10MΩ]──→ HV RAIL
;;       |    3: ISENSE (current)       ←──[0.1Ω]──→ SOURCE
;;       |    4: RT/CT (timing)         ←──[10kΩ]──→ [1nF]──GND
;;       |    5: GND                    ←──GND
;;       |    6: OUTPUT                ──→ IRF840 GATE
;;       |    7: VCC                   ←──+12V (10µF decoupling)
;;       |    8: VREF                  ──→ 5V ref
;;       |                             |
;;       +-----------------------------+
;;
;;         IRF840 (N-ch, 500V, 8A)
;;         D = DRAIN ──→ Flyback primary dot
;;         G = GATE ───→ UC3843 pin 6 (via 10Ω + 12V zener to GND)
;;         S = SOURCE ─→ [0.1Ω 1W] ──→ GND
;;                           |
;;                           +──→ UC3843 pin 3 (current sense)
;;
;;         Flyback Transformer (DIY or Coilcraft DA2034)
;;         =============================================
;;         Core: EE25 or EPCOS E25 (N87 ferrite)
;;         Primary:   6 turns, 0.5mm magnet wire
;;         Secondary: 180 turns, 0.15mm triple-insulated wire
;;         Aux:       6 turns, 0.3mm (for UC3843 VCC if needed)
;;         Gap:       0.1mm paper spacer (for energy storage)
;;         Inductance: ~200µH primary
;;
;;         Primary circuit:
;;         +12V ──→ Primary (non-dot) ──→ IRF840 DRAIN
;;                                        |
;;                                   Primary (dot) ──→ GND
;;
;;         Secondary circuit:
;;         Secondary (dot) ──→ [MUR460] ──→ +HV RAIL
;;         Secondary (non-dot) ──→ GND
;;
;;         Snubber (critical for flyback):
;;         Across primary: [100pF 1kV] + [10kΩ] in series + [UF4007]
;;         (or RCD snubber: 47kΩ + 1nF + UF4007)
;;
;;         +HV RAIL
;;            |
;;       +----v----+
;;       | 10µF    |  ← 450V or 630V electrolytic, LOW ESR
;;       | 450V    |
;;       +----+----+
;;            |
;;       +----v----+    +---------+
;;       | 100kΩ   |    |  LED    |  ← HV indicator (with 100kΩ + 5V zener)
;;       | 2W      |    | (red)   |
;;       +----+----+    +----+----+
;;            |              |
;;            +--------------+──→ GND (bleeder + indicator)
;;
;;         Expected HV: 100V–500V adjustable via feedback divider
;;         (use potentiometer in feedback for tuning)
;;
;; PULSE GENERATION SECTION
;; =======================
;;
;;         TRIGGER INPUT (BNC, from Red Pitaya DIO or function generator)
;;              |
;;         +----v----+
;;         | 50Ω     |  ← Termination
;;         | RTERM   |
;;         +----+----+
;;              |
;;         +----v--------+
;;         |              |
;;         |  TC4427CPA   |  ← Dual gate driver, 1.5A output
;;         |  (using     |
;;         |   channel A) |
;;         |  Pin 2: IN A ←── TRIGGER
;;         |  Pin 7: OUT A──→ [10Ω] ──→ IRF830 GATE
;;         |  Pin 1: VDD  ←── +12V
;;         |  Pin 3: GND  ←── GND
;;         +--------------+
;;
;;         IRF830PBF (N-ch, 500V, 4.5A) — SAME PART AS V5!
;;         ================================================
;;         G = GATE ←── [10Ω] ──→ TC4427 OUT A
;;                      |
;;                 [12V zener] ←──→ GND (gate protection, clamps to 12V)
;;                      |
;;                 [100kΩ] ←──→ GND (pull-down, ensures OFF when idle)
;;         D = DRAIN ──→ Pulse transformer PRIMARY (dot)
;;         S = SOURCE ──→ GND
;;
;;         Pulse Transformer (same as V5, but uprated insulation)
;;         =======================================================
;;         Core: T68-2 toroid (Amidon #68 material, red/yellow paint)
;;         Primary:   12 turns, 26 AWG triple-insulated
;;         Secondary: 12 turns, 26 AWG triple-insulated
;;         Insulation: 2 layers Kapton tape between windings
;;         Hi-pot test: 1kV DC, 1 minute, <1µA
;;
;;         +HV RAIL (100–400V)
;;              |
;;         +----v-----------------+
;;         |                      |
;;         |   Pulse Xfmr         |
;;         |   Primary (dot)    |←── IRF830 DRAIN
;;         |   Primary (GND)    |──→ [10µF 450V] ──→ +HV (damping cap)
;;         |                      |
;;         |   Secondary (dot)    |──→ [MUR460] ──→ BNC CENTER (OUTPUT)
;;         |   Secondary (GND)  |──→ BNC SHIELD ──→ GND
;;         |                      |
;;         +----------------------+
;;
;;         Damping / Protection on Output:
;;         BNC CENTER ──→ [MUR460] ──→ GND (reverse biased, catches negative spikes)
;;         BNC CENTER ──→ [100pF 1kV] ──→ GND (RF suppression)
;;
;; OUTPUT
;; ======
;;
;;         BNC CONNECTOR (female, panel mount)
;;         Center: Pulse transformer secondary (via MUR460)
;;         Shield: GND
;;         ↓
;;         50Ω coax (RG58 or RG174) to V5 TX_IN SMA
;;
;; CONTROL INTERFACE
;; =================
;;
;;         Arduino Nano (optional, for pulse parameter control)
;;         ================================================
;;         D9  (PWM) ──→ TC4427 IN A (via 1kΩ)
;;         D13 (LED) ──→ Status LED
;;         A0  (ADC) ──→ HV rail monitor (via 1000:1 divider: 10MΩ + 10kΩ)
;;         5V  ──→ TC4427 VDD (or separate 12V from MT3608)
;;         GND ──→ Common ground
;;
;;         Serial commands (115200 baud):
;;         p100   → pulse width = 100 µs
;;         f100   → PRF = 100 Hz
;;         b10    → burst = 10 pulses
;;         v200   → set voltage = 200V (adjusts flyback feedback if digital pot fitted)
;;         s      → single burst
;;         r      → run continuous
;;         x      → stop
;;         h      → read HV rail voltage
;;
;; SAFETY CIRCUITS
;; ===============
;;
;;         1. BLEEDER (always connected across HV cap)
;;            +HV ──→ [100kΩ 2W] ──→ GND
;;            Time constant: τ = RC = 100kΩ × 10µF = 1 second
;;            Discharge to <50V: ~5 seconds
;;
;;         2. HV INDICATOR
;;            +HV ──→ [1MΩ] ──→ [5V zener] ──→ [LED 2mA] ──→ GND
;;            LED lights when HV > 5V + LED drop (~7V total)
;;            Visible warning that cap is charged
;;
;;         3. INTERLOCK (optional)
;;            Enclosure lid switch ──→ UC3843 VCC
;;            Lid open = UC3843 unpowered = no HV generation
;;            (Does NOT discharge cap — bleeder does that)
;;
;;         4. FUSE
;;            +7.4V ──→ [FUSE 3A fast] ──→ rest of circuit
;;
;;         5. TVS (on TX output, protects against transducer kickback)
;;            BNC CENTER ──→ [P6KE400A] ──→ GND
;;            (400V TVS, clamps transients)
;;

;; PCB LAYOUT HINTS
;; ================
;; - Flyback transformer: Place near board edge, away from logic
;; - HV cap: Right next to transformer secondary diode
;; - IRF840: Heat sink or copper pour, near transformer
;; - Bleeder resistor: Physically large, clearly visible (safety)
;; - BNC output: Board edge, isolated from logic section by 5mm slot
;; - TC4427: Near IRF830, short gate trace (<20mm)
;; - UC3843: Near IRF840, short traces to gate and current sense
;; - 12V pre-boost (MT3608): Corner of board, feeds UC3843 and TC4427
;; - No sensitive traces under flyback or HV cap
;; - Ground: Star point at UC3843 GND pin. Separate HV_GND and LOGIC_GND joined at one point.
;;
;; TEST POINTS
;; ===========
;; TP1: +12V (MT3608 output) — verify pre-boost
;; TP2: UC3843 VCC — verify controller powered
;; TP3: IRF840 GATE — verify gate drive (scope)
;; TP4: IRF840 DRAIN — verify switching (scope, HV probe or 100:1 divider)
;; TP5: +HV RAIL — verify output voltage (DMM, 1000V rated)
;; TP6: BNC OUTPUT — verify pulse shape (scope with 100:1 probe or attenuator)
;;
;; ASSEMBLY ORDER
;; =============
;; 1. Solder low-voltage section first (UC3843, TC4427, MT3608, resistors, small caps)
;; 2. Power on, verify 12V rail, verify 5V rail
;; 3. Solder IRF840 (no transformer yet), verify gate drive
;; 4. Solder flyback transformer, HV diode, HV cap
;; 5. First power-on with HV: use current-limited supply (500mA), verify ~100V output
;; 6. Gradually increase load, tune feedback divider for desired voltage
;; 7. Solder IRF830 pulse section, pulse transformer, output BNC
;; 8. Test pulse output into 50Ω load (start at low voltage, work up)
;; 9. Final test: 400V pulse, verify shape, verify no ringing
;; 10. Safety check: bleeder, LED, interlock all functional
;;

### Experimental Design Recommendations
- Minimum SNR: 25 dB for reliable G₀ recovery
- Optimal frequency: 0.5-2× corner frequency (300-1200 Hz for τ_σ≈0.27 ms)
- Receiver spacing: ≤λ/4 (≤0.2 cm for 150 Hz, c≈1.4 m/s)
- Array aperture: ≥2λ (≥1.9 cm)
- Geometry: Log-spaced or random (avoid uniform/endfire)

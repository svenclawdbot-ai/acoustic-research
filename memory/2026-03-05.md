## Engineering Research: Catching Hand Mechanism

Deep dive into engineering problems related to robotic/prosthetic catching hands.

### Topics Covered
- **Grasp mechanics** — Force distribution, grip stability during impact
- **Material selection** — Compliance vs. rigidity trade-offs for catch absorption
- **Sensor integration** — Real-time grip adjustment based on object velocity/mass
- **Actuation systems** — Rapid response requirements for dynamic catching
- **Control algorithms** — Predictive models for trajectory interception

### Key Engineering Challenges Identified
1. **Impact dampening** — Dissipating kinetic energy without losing grip
2. **Adaptive compliance** — Variable stiffness based on object properties
3. **Reaction time** — Sub-100ms response for fast-moving objects
4. **Tactile feedback** — Pressure sensing for slip prevention
5. **Power density** — Compact actuation with sufficient force output

### Research References
- Soft robotics actuation methods
- Variable impedance control in robotic hands
- Biomimetic gripper designs (human hand biomechanics)
- Impact-absorbing composite materials

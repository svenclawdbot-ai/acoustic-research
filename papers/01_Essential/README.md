# Essential Papers - Download Links

## Priority 1: Read These First (Week 1)

### 1. Sarvazyan 1998 - THE Foundation Paper
**Title:** Shear wave elasticity imaging: A new ultrasonic technology of medical diagnostics  
**Authors:** Sarvazyan AP, Rudenko OV, Swanson SD, Fowlkes JB, Emelianov SY  
**Journal:** Ultrasound in Medicine & Biology, 1998  
**DOI:** 10.1016/S0301-5629(98)00110-0

**Download Options:**
- ResearchGate: https://www.researchgate.net/publication/12913173
- PubMed: https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/10385964/
- ScienceDirect: https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S0301562998001100

**Key Equation:** G = ρc_s²

---

### 2. Nightingale 2002 - ARFI Imaging
**Title:** Acoustic Radiation Force Impulse Imaging: In Vivo Demonstration of Clinical Feasibility  
**Authors:** Nightingale K, Soo MS, Nightingale R, Trahey G  
**Journal:** Ultrasound in Medicine & Biology, 2002  
**DOI:** 10.1016/S0301-5629(02)00456-4

**Download:** Search via your institutional library

**Key Concept:** Using ultrasound itself to generate shear waves

---

### 3. Chen 2009 - SDUV (Multi-Frequency)
**Title:** Shearwave Dispersion Ultrasound Vibrometry (SDUV) for Measuring Tissue Elasticity and Viscosity  
**Authors:** Chen S, Urban MW, Pislaru C, et al.  
**Journal:** IEEE Trans. UFFC, 2009  
**DOI:** 10.1109/TUFFC.2009.1005

**Download Options:**
- ResearchGate: https://www.researchgate.net/publication/24004688
- IEEE Xplore: https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/4815178

**Key Concept:** Multi-frequency measurement separates G' and G''

---

### 4. Parker 2014 - Viscoelastic Models
**Title:** The Inconvenient Nature of Soft Tissue Viscosity: Why It Matters and What to Do About It  
**Authors:** Parker KJ  
**Journal:** Ultrasound in Medicine & Biology, 2014  
**DOI:** 10.1016/j.ultrasmedbio.2014.05.021

**Download:** Check author website or institutional access

**Key Concept:** KV model is sufficient; don't overcomplicate

---

### 5. Barr 2022 - Clinical Viscosity Relevance
**Title:** Shear Wave Viscosity: A Quantitative Biomarker for Liver Inflammation and Fibrosis  
**Authors:** Barr RG, Ferraioli G, Palmeri ML, et al.  
**Journal:** Radiology, 2022  
**DOI:** 10.1148/radiol.210475

**Download:** Radiology website or institutional access

**Key Finding:** Viscosity distinguishes inflammation from fibrosis

NOISE BUDGET ANALYSIS SUMMARY
==================================================

Hardware: STM32 + AD9833 + Switchable TIA (1k/10k)
Target: Soil impedance spectroscopy, 1 Hz – 1 MHz

KEY FINDINGS:
- 12-bit ADC is sufficient for 1% accuracy
- System is quantization-noise limited
- TIA gain of 1-10 kΩ optimal
- Total sweep time: ~40-60 seconds for 50 points
- Cost: ~£15 (MCU) + £5 (DDS) + £3 (TIA) = ~£23
